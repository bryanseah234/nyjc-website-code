How to get to NY

NYJC is located at 128 Serangoon Avenue 3 Singapore 556111.
(Tel: +65 62842281 Fax: +65 62855147)

By MRT


From Choice of transportation to Nanyang JC
Ang Mo Kio MRT Station Bus No. 22, 24, 73, 133, 135, 136
Bishan MRT Station Bus No. 53, 58
Serangoon MRT Station Bus No. 22, 53, 105, 534,
By foot (approx 8 mins)
Lorong Chuan MRT Station (Circle Line) 2-minute walk from the station to NYJC.

The station is 1 stop from the Bishan MRT Interchange (which is connected to North-South MRT Line). The station is also 1 stop from the Serangoon MRT Interchange (which is connected to North-East MRT Line).
